Welcome to Crust N Crumb, where every bite is crafted with care and passion. We are a small, locally-owned bakery dedicated to bringing you the finest, freshly baked bread, pastries, and sweets. From the warmth of our oven to your table, we use only the highest quality ingredients, with a focus on local and sustainable products whenever possible.

At Crust N Crumb, we believe in the art of baking. Whether it’s the comforting aroma of a golden baguette or the decadence of a freshly iced cupcake, our treats are designed to bring joy to every moment. Our breads are handmade daily, with a variety of flavors that range from rustic, hearty loaves to soft, sweet breads. Our pastries and cakes are baked from scratch, with love and attention to detail, ensuring that every experience is a delightful one.

We take pride in creating a welcoming space where the simple pleasures of freshly baked goods meet community, conversation, and warmth. Whether you’re here for your morning coffee and croissant or to grab a loaf of artisan bread for dinner, we’re here to make every visit a little sweeter.

Thank you for supporting our bakery. We’re thrilled to share our love of baking with you!
